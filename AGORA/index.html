What is AGORA 2.0?

AGORA 2.0 is an annual Hackathon organized by IEDC-JEC for students of Jyothi Engineering College. This hackathon is organized in association with
Google Developer Students Club(GDSC-JEC),Jyothi Engineering College.

FAQs
Is this hackathon an online event or offline?
AGORA 2.0 is a combo of online and offline event.
Do this hackathon has any participation fees?
No. The hackathon is free of cost for each and every student from Jyothi Engineering College,Thrissur.
What are all the restrictions on Team formation for the event?
A team can have at most 5 members and minimum of 2 members.
What benefits will this Hackathon provide?
Hackathon provide hands on experience with the mates having same passion.Each and every participant will get a participation certificate.
